About Us

We are Documentary Nepal, We produce and help you produce Movies, Documentaries, TV Series, TV Commercials, Music Videos and many more, We are a group experienced individuals who have worked on dozens of national and international projects, Our knowledge, experience and ability makes us the perfect fit for your next project. We undertake both National and International projects.

Our Story

Documentary Nepal has been collaborating with an increasing number of film crews from all over the world. Our experienced and highly-skilled staff provides technical support and facilitates film crews to unveil Nepal's hidden stories, unheard realities and incredible diversity.

Humble Beginnings.

Our journey started in 2062 BS. (2005 AD.) with Amrit Pudasaini, Mr Amrit has experience working as a program coordinator, program producer, executive producer and brings his vast experience and knowledge to our team. Mr Pudasaini brought our team together in 2010, Since then we have not looked back and have worked on many known international and national brands like Samsonite TVC . We've made a handful documentaries on child labor and we've also worked on many tourism related documentaries. Mr Amrit has also worked on many international films and telenovelas like Up in the Wind (Chinese) and Joia Rara (Brazilian).

Our Vision

Our skill set and our team grows with every project we undertake. We as a team want to reach great heights with film making in Nepal. We would love for you to be a part of our journey, Our team with its unique combination of both youth and experience has brought us much success and we hope it will continue doing so.

Our Policies

We only have a few policies around here, but we really enforce them.

Health and Safety

For who has health has hope; for who has hope has everything.

Film production can be a physically and mentally demanding process, and it is important to take steps to prevent accidents and injuries that could disrupt the production or cause harm to individuals.

Quality control

Quality is not an act, it is a habit.

Quality control is an important policy when filming with us, it helps to ensure that the final product meets the highest standards of quality and meets the needs and expectations of the client.

Privacy

Privacy is Power, We are fiercely protective of your privacy.

Privacy is essential when filming abroad, as it helps to protect the personal information and confidentiality of individuals involved in the film production and ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

Environmental sustainability

We try to leave the earth a better place than when we arrived.

Environmental sustainability is essential when filming abroad, as it helps to minimize the environmental impact of the film production, promote sustainable practices, and ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations.

Cultural Respect

Disrespect towards the locals and our nation will not be tolerated.

Cultural respect is essential when filming abroad in foreign nations like Nepal, as it helps to build positive relationships with the local community, ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations, and enhance the professional reputation of the film crew and production company.

Equipment Maintenance

We make sure your equipments are properly taken care of and maintained.

Equipment Maintenance is an important consideration when filming abroad in foreign nations like Nepal, as it helps to ensure that the film crew has access to the necessary tools and technologies to produce their film and that these tools are in good working order.
