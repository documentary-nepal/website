Our Services

We are able to fixed projects, from simple location scouting, to complete film production facilities and service, including scouting, casting, location permits, crew and equipment, hotel booking, travel and a lot more.

We are willing to offer you:

Fixer

Documentary Nepal has the best fixers in Nepal. It is important to hire a fixer when filming in a foreign country because a fixer can help facilitate the logistics of filming in a foreign country and act as a liaison between the film crew and the local community. Our fixers can help navigate local customs and cultural norms to ensure that the film crew is respectful and sensitive to the local community.

01.

Filming Crew

All our team members have hundreds of years in combined experience, and our team has been working with Documentary Nepal for more than 10 years on average. Our filming crew & team members are specialists in their own chosen sectors and would be more than glad to provide and give advice while filming in Nepal.

02.

Filming Permits

It is necessary for you to acquire the “National Filming Permit” which grants you the permission to film on non-private or non-special locations. Obtaining filming permits is an important step in the production process when filming in a foreign country, helping to ensure that the film crew is in compliance with local laws and regulations, and that the necessary precautions are taken to ensure the safety and well-being of all involved.

03.

Skilled Line Producers

All of our dedicated line producers are highly skilled and have very sound knowledge of the process of film making. They play a crucial role in ensuring that a film is produced efficiently and within budget. They are also responsible for creating and managing the production schedule, ensuring that all necessary tasks are completed on time and on budget.

04.

Transportation

Transportation services can help to ensure that the film crew has reliable and convenient access to the locations where they will be filming.Transportation services can help to ensure the safety of the film crew, especially when filming in areas that may not be familiar to them. Professional drivers who are familiar with local conditions and routes can help to ensure that the film crew arrives at their destinations safely and efficiently.

05.

Location Scouting

Nepal is a country with a wide range of landscapes, from the Himalayan mountains to subtropical forests, and location scouting is necessary to identify suitable locations for filming that meet the specific needs of the production.Location scouting also helps to assess the logistics and safety of filming in a particular location. This can include identifying any potential challenges such as access to power and water, as well as any potential risks such as unstable terrain or inclement weather.

06.

Food

Providing food for the film crew is essential for ensuring that they have access to nutritious meals that will help them stay healthy and energized during long and demanding days of filming.Providing food on set can also help to increase efficiency by minimizing the need for the film crew to take breaks to go out and find food. This can help to keep the production on schedule and within budget. Our team is properly equipped to handle catering/food services for all types of teams, Big or Small.

07.

Proper Communication

All our team members are fluent in English and some of them are also familiar with other foreign languages. Proper communication is essential for overcoming these barriers and ensuring that the film crew is able to communicate effectively with locals.Proper communication is essential for coordinating the various tasks and activities involved in film production, such as location scouting, casting, and rehearsals. Without effective communication, it can be difficult to ensure that everyone is on the same page and working towards the same goals.

08.

Film Equipment Rental

We have a large database of filming equipment in Nepal, Film equipment may not always be readily available in every country, and renting equipment can ensure that the film crew has access to the necessary tools and technologies to produce their film. film equipment rental is an important resource for filmmakers when filming in a foreign nation like Nepal, as it helps to ensure that they have access to the necessary tools and technologies to produce their film in an efficient and cost-effective manner.
